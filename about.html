About Us

Clara's Cuisine was founded by the desire to create healthy, mouth-watering culinary delights for everyone. We take pride in delivering excellent services that leaves our customers satisfied.

Clara's Cuisine sources for only the freshest locally grown vegetables to ensure a memorable dining experience for you.
We are the perfect place for a romantic meal for two, a catch-up with friends, family parties or conferences. With a comfortable surrounding, an exquisite view of the water-front, affordable price and a sitting for up to 50 guests, we can cater for your occasions.

Our Goals

Clara's Cuisine aims to sell delicious and remarkable food and beverages that meet the highest quality, freshness and seasonality criteria, while combining modern-creative and classic cooking traditions. By showcasing warmth, graciousness, efficiency of service, skill, professionalism and integrity in our work, we will continually serve our customers with exceptional service.

We hope to have every customer who comes through our doors leave impressed by their experience at our Cuisine and excited to come back again.

Fresh, tasty dishes just for your delight and satisfaction.
